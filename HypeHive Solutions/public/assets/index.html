Target Audience & Strategy

Who We Help and How We Grow Them

HypeHive Solutions focuses on small businesses and startups that need stronger online visibility, consistent content, and a clear brand style to attract customers.

Target Audience

  • Small business owners who do not have time to manage social media daily
  • Startups that need a professional first impression online
  • Online sellers who need consistent logo, color, and post design
  • F&B, fashion, beauty, and lifestyle brands that depend on visual content

Main Social Media Platform

Instagram is selected as the main platform because it is visual, mobile-friendly, and suitable for posts, reels, stories, product promotions, and customer engagement.

  • Platform: Instagram
  • Handle: @hypehvie
